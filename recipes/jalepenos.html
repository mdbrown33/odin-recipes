Atomic Jalepeno Bites

Description

I am someone who loves a little bit of heat. These jalepeno bites hit the spot when you want a spicy treat. Plus you only need three ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Jalepeno peppers
  • Cream cheese
  • Bacon or bacon bits
  • Optional: cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Slice jalepenos in half lengthwise. Scoop out all the seeds (leave a few seeds if you are brave).
  3. Fill jalepeno halves with cream cheese. Optional: Mix cayenne pepper or hot sauce into the cream cheese before filling. How much is up to your taste.
  4. Wrap each jalepeno half in bacon, or if using bacon bits press them into the cream cheese.
  5. Place halves on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Bake for 20-24 minutes, until cream cheese is golden and bacon is cooked.
